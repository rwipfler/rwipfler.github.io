I'm a fifth-year PhD candidate at Caltech in the Orphan Lab, working on the biogeochemistry of deep-sea methane seeps. My dissertation focuses on measuring microbial metabolic activity in situ — at pressure, in the dark, on the seafloor — using custom high-pressure incubation systems I've been building and deploying from research vessels.

I'm also an Ocean Exploration Trust alumni, having joined E/V Nautilus first as an intern and later as Science Manager in Training.
