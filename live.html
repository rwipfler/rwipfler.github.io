How to use this page

This page is meant to point people toward official, stable sources for deep-sea livestreams and recent dive footage. Some programs stream continuously during active expeditions, while others cycle between live operations, archived dives, and highlights.

For stability, I recommend embedding at most one featured video and linking out to official hubs for everything else.

Featured embed slot

Replace this box with a single YouTube embed once you decide which source you want to feature most often. I would use this for Nautilus Live or a current Schmidt Ocean dive when one is active.
